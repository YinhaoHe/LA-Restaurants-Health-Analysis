How many restaurants do inspectors visit everyday?

Data Explanation

  • March 10,2019

Number of facilities visited per day

Start from 2016-06-01 to 2018-12-31, the data shows how many restaurants do inspectors visited in LA every day. The number in each column only shows the total amounts of restaurants in different health categories. From the flow of the chart, there seems to have an unstable number of inspections completed every day. Suggestions are stated below:

  • For government use, it helps to identify the overall workload done by inspectors per day.
  • For commercial use, it helps to identify when to prepare for the upcoming inspections.
