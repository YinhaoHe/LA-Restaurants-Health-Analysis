We Are Group Reataurant Health Analysis

The overall health analysis of LA restaurants will help the government find out the most high-risk areas in the food industry, which means that they can strengthen the supervision of related issues for prevention. It allows the government to build a much more perfect health evaluation system to inspect these restaurants. Also, the geographical distribution analysis of the restaurants can provide some suggestions for the people who what to open a restaurant. By analyzing the current restaurants, we are able to know the food preference of people in LA.
