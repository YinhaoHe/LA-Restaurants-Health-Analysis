Data Explanation

  • March 10,2019

Risk Distribution

This chart shows the numbers of restaurants in each risk level and their percentage of market share. Permits in the restaurants are now based on risk levels, which are determined by type of food and preparation steps. In general, the more complex the menu/preparation of foods are, the higher the risk level.

Suggestions are stated below:

  • For government use, it helps to generalize the restaurants and to suggest additional/special inspections due to different risk level.
  • For commercial use, it helps to provide basic understanding of the current market, and shows potential business in the futrue.
